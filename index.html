THE BITEW FOUNDATION (TBF)
The purpose of the foundation is: Basic education, social activism and development in Ethiopia

Who we are?

TBF works with a variety of projects, partners and community groups in many educational and development-related endeavors. Our mission is not only to ensure that every child has access to a meaningful education, but also to ensure that children’s nutrition and healthcare needs are met. We provide support to various partners to make our community impact more tangible, contributing to movements that address areas of need such as women’s issues and community awareness issues.

Our support in each of these areas not only helps individual efforts, but also guides our work in terms of how we invest our energies as an organization. It helps us realize the problems faced by different communities and the important role played by each type of endeavor. Given below are links to TBF projects based on the project type.

TBF’S ACTIVITIES

Up until now, TBF has focused on clarifying, identifying, and researching needs as well as funding and sustaining a few core projects. TBF raises funds for its projects in a number of ways, including requesting individual and corporate donations, selling merchandise (T-shirts, calendars, greetings cards, etc.), fundraising dinners, hosting Ethiopian classical music and dance concerts, hosting popular music concerts and many others.

A positive outcome of our fundraising efforts has been an increased awareness of Ethiopian culture in the United States. Our foundation has helped develop study groups and speaker series, which are working towards forming networks for various grassroots volunteers involved with non-governmental organizations.

If you would like to find out more about TBF and our activities, or if you would like to participate and contribute to our efforts in any way, please contact us. We invite you to join the movement!
